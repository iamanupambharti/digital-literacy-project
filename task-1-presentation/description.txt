Tool Used: Canva

Design Overview:

The infographic that I created is intended to provide information to the student community in an easy and understandable way about what digital literacy is. I have divided the information into three parts, which is easy to read and understand.



The first part is related to what digital literacy is. It is defined as the ability to find information from the internet, understand if the information is correct or not, and share the information using digital tools.

The second part is related to the tools that are available for the student community. I have mentioned the tools that are available for the student community, such as Google Docs, Notion, Canva, Figma, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. These tools are very helpful for the student community in completing their work and working in groups.

The third part is related to the safety of the internet. It provides information to the student community in an easy way, such as creating a strong password, checking the privacy settings, and not clicking on suspicious links.


Reflection:

While creating the infographic using Canva, I tried to keep it simple and easy to understand because I am a student digital ambassador.
Another challenge that I encountered is what to emphasize most. However, through this, I realized that visual design is not just about making it look good, but also about making sure people notice the most important part first.
Another challenge is what tools to use, as there are many to choose from. However, I chose the ones that are most frequently used by students and can be applied in individual and group work.
From the activity, I realized that digital literacy is not just about technology, but also about using it in a smart and safe way.
