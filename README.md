   ## Project Overview
   This repository contains my comprehensive portfolio for the Digital Literacy project. The objective of this project was to develop and demonstrate essential digital skills required in the      modern academic and professional world. Throughout the five tasks, I have explored visual communication, professional networking, technical platform engagement, digital etiquette, and cybersecurity awareness.

   The project reflects my journey in becoming a "Student Digital Ambassador," focusing not just on using technology, but on using it smartly, safely, and effectively.

   ---

   ## Module Summaries

   ### 🛠️ Task 1: Visual Communication & Infographics
   In this module, I focused on the power of visual data representation. I used **Canva** to design an infographic tailored for the student community.
   - **Key Focus:** Defining digital literacy, highlighting essential student tools (Notion, Figma, Google Meet), and promoting internet safety.
   - **Reflection:** I learned that effective design is about hierarchy—ensuring the most critical information catches the eye first.
   - **Artifacts:** `task-1-presentation/infographic..png`

   ### 🌐 Task 2: Building a Professional Digital Presence
   This task involved transitioning from a casual internet user to a professional digital citizen. I established and optimized profiles on three major platforms:
   - **GitHub:** For version control and showcasing my coding projects.
   - **LinkedIn:** For professional networking and career opportunities.
   - **Stack Overflow:** For collaborative problem-solving and technical learning.
   - **Artifacts:** Screenshots and reflection notes in `task-2-portfolio/`

   ### 📊 Task 3: Digital Platforms & Data Tools
   Here, I explored cloud-based collaboration and technical skill-building platforms:
   - **HackerRank:** Completed basic programming challenges to get used to technical interview environments.
   - **Google Workspace:** Created a 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' using Google Forms and analyzed the data via Google Sheets.
   - **Artifacts:** Code challenge proof and form responses in `task-3-platforms/`

   ### 📧 Task 4: Professional Etiquette & Social Media
   Communication is the backbone of digital literacy. This module covered:
   - **Email Etiquette:** Drafting professional emails for internship inquiries and academic extensions.
   - **Social Media Management:** Developing a "Do’s and Don’ts" checklist to maintain a clean and professional digital footprint.
   - **Artifacts:** Email drafts and the `social-media-checklist.md` in `task-4-email-etiquette/`

   ### 🛡️ Task 5: Cybersecurity & Digital Safety
   The final module focused on protecting oneself in the digital space. I conducted a case study on **Academic Phishing Attacks**, a common threat to university students.
   - **Case Study:** Analyzed how spoofed emails trick students into revealing credentials and the potential consequences of such breaches.
   - **Prevention:** Created a checklist for staying safe against social engineering.
   - **Artifacts:** `case-study.md` and `prevention-checklist.md` in `task-5-cybercrime/`
