**Digital Literacy Project**

This repository holds all the documentation and artifacts for the Digital Literacy Project, which focuses on the essential skills needed to interact in the digital world in a safe and effective way.

**Project Structure**

The project is divided into five main tasks, as follows:

**Task 1: Presentation & Infographic**

* Goal: Create an infographic about digital literacy for students in an easy-to-understand format.
* Tools Used: Canva.
* Content: Will cover digital literacy, essential tools for students (Notion, Figma, etc.), and internet safety.
* Files: task-1-presentation/

**Task 2: Digital Portfolio & Professional Presence**

* Goal: Create a professional online presence.
* Platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, Stack Overflow.
* Focus: Creating a portfolio of work, professional networking, and collaborative learning.
* Files: task-2-portfolio/

**Task 3: Platform Activities**

* Goal: Interact with digital platforms and coding challenges.
* Files: task-3-platforms/

**Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media**

* Goal: Understand professional email etiquette and appropriate use of social media.
* Content: Will include sample professional emails, an extensive Social Media Checklist (Do’s and Don’ts).
* Files: task-4-email-etiquette/

**Task 5: Cybercrime & Prevention**

* Goal: Understand digital crime, threats, and prevention.
* Content: Will include a case study on Academic Phishing Attacks, prevention checklist.
* Files: task-5-cybercrime/
