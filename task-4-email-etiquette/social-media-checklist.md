# Social Media Checklist

## Social Media Do's ✅

*   **Privacy First:** Regularly audit your account’s privacy settings to manage who views your personal posts and information.
*   **Think Before Posting:** Take a moment to consider whether the information you are posting is appropriate for a future employer or internship coordinator to view.
*   **Engage Professionally:** Utilize your accounts to promote your technical projects, academic achievements, or interesting field-related articles.
*   **Verify Information:** Research and verify the information in news articles or shared links to ensure you are not spreading misinformation to your network.
*   **Network Authentically:** Network with alumni and professionals by sending personalized and polite messages rather than random connection requests.

## Social Media Don'ts ❌

*   **Oversharing:** Avoid posting personal information such as your home address, location, and personal contact information.
*   **Public Venting:** Never post complaints and negative comments regarding your professors, college, and previous workplaces.
*   **Engage in Conflict:** Avoid engaging in flame wars and arguments that may harm your digital reputation.
*   **Ignore Security:** Don’t use the same password across all accounts and don’t click on suspicious direct messages containing links.
*   **Neglect Your Profile:** Don’t neglect to update and maintain information on platforms such as LinkedIn that you use to network professionally.
